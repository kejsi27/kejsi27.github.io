Mobile Banking is the ultimate in on-demand service. With it, you can receive alerts about your account activity delivered right to your cell phone.

Imagine getting a text message if your account falls below $100. Or, receive an alert so you'll know as soon as a deposit has been made, or a check has cleared. Mobile Banking delivers the information you need, when you need it.

The New and Improved Mobile Banking Features Include:

  • Combined Personal and Business Mobile Application
  • Bill Pay
  • Mobile Capture which will allow you to deposit checks directly from your phone with a touch of a button
  • Transfer funds between accounts
  • Money Management tool helps you simplify how you manage your personal finances
  • Locate your nearest People's Bank branch including ATMs and hours
  • Contact us with the touch of a button
  • View your recent transaction history
  • Turn your debit card on/off with a touch of a button
  • Person to Person Payments (P2P) which will allow you to send money to your friends and family
